== Description ==
This plugin lets you add a random Quote to your WordPress page. Select your favorite category - Art Quote, Beauty Quote, Friendship Quote, Future Quote, Happiness Quote, Hope Quote, Inspirational Quote, Leadership Quote, Life Quote, Love Quote, Morning Quote, Motivational Quote, Parenting Quote, Positive Quote, Romantic Quote, Success Quote, Time Quote, Travel Quote, Trust Quote, Truth Quote - and famous quotations will automatically appear on your page, every day!

Can't decide which topic you like best? No problem! You can place multiple QuoteTour widgets on your page and enjoy several topic feeds. Our top 20 topic feeds contain family-friendly quotes by famous authors that are funny, inspirational, motivational and wise.

[QuoteTour](https://www.quotetour.com) we are working day and night to provide you beautiful quotes every day...

If you have any suggestions for a new add-on, feel free to email me at info@quotetour.com.

= Features =
* Widget allows you to place a Random Quote on your page
* Widget adapts to the theme of your page
* Widget has multiple different categories to choose from
* Plugin allows you to place multiple widgets on your page

== Installation ==
1. Upload `quotetour-widget.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress.
1. Go to `Appearance`,  `Widgets` in WordPress.
1. Drag the `QuoteTour Widget` to the sidebar, for example the `Main Widget Area` or the `Secondary Widget Area`.
1. Optionally, select the category of quotes you would like to see, change the title and decide if you want to show the quote author.
